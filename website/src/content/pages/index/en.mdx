    ## FAQ

    *Rolebase answers your questions*

    Have questions about Rolebase, how it works, or what it can do? Here are the most frequently asked questions. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact us!
  </Fragment>

  <Fragment slot="right">
    <FaqItem question="What about Rolebase for nonprofits?">
      We offer significant discounts or completely free access for nonprofits depending on your status.

      [Get in touch](/en/contact) with us to learn more.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Who uses Rolebase?">
      Organizations of all types and sizes use Rolebase to bring more transparency and collaboration to their teams.

      Agencies of 35 people ([Lonestone](https://lonestone.io/), Yeswedev, Troopers, IDS Media) or over 150 people (Evea Scop), startups, nonprofits, cooperatives...
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Can I suggest changes to the tool?">
      Yes, absolutely!

      * You can get in touch with us via the [Contact page](/en/contact).
      * If you're a developer, you can fork the [official GitHub repository](https://github.com/Godefroy/rolebase) and submit a pull request or a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/Godefroy/rolebase/issues).
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Can I self-host a Rolebase instance?">
      Yes, the [Rolebase source code](https://github.com/Godefroy/rolebase) is available under an open source license, and you can host it yourself. Server costs will be your responsibility and you will have no warranty or support.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Is the confidentiality of my data guaranteed?">
      Only the people you have invited to your organization can access it.

      Data is stored in Europe in a secure datacenter at Nhost.

      The Rolebase team can only access it with your authorization or for purely technical purposes.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="What integrations are available?">
      You can sync your Rolebase calendar with any calendar app using an iCal subscription link.

      We plan to add Google Calendar, Outlook Calendar, Slack, Zapier integrations and many more. Share your needs to help us prioritize!
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Does Rolebase integrate with third-party applications?">
      Rolebase can easily integrate with your usual tools through API connections.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="What languages is Rolebase available in?">
      Rolebase is available in French and English.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="What is Rolebase's business model?">
      Rolebase is a SaaS (Software as a Service) with monthly and annual per-user subscriptions.

      This subscription includes access to the platform as well as personalized support. Our source code is fully available on GitHub under the MIT license for companies wanting on-premise deployment.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Is Rolebase GDPR compliant?">
      Rolebase complies with the data protection framework established by GDPR principles.

      Our servers are located in Europe and we do not transfer any data outside European territory.
    </FaqItem>

    <FaqItem question="Can I enforce Holacracy-style rules?">
      Yes, and you choose how much structure to enforce. Rolebase offers three [governance modes](/en/docs/governance-modes): Free lets every member edit the org chart, Agile gives each role to its lead, and Strict routes every structure change through a proposal and a shared decision, the way Holacracy and similar frameworks work. You can switch modes at any time as your organization matures.
    </FaqItem>
  </Fragment>
